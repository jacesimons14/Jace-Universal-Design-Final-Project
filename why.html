Why Universal Design?

The web has constantly been evolving. Fundamental shifts have always come from grassroots movements demanding change:

  • CSS Zen Garden showed us the power of Design.
  • Web Standards taught us the importance of Code.
  • HTML5 Semantics gave meaning to our Content.

Adding our voice for Universal Design is the next logical step in making information available for everyone and achieving the full potential of the internet.
